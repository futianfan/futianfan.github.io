Brief Introduction

Tianfan Fu currently is a PhD candidate under the supervision of Prof. Jimeng Sun at Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Georgia Institute of Technology. He received his B.S. and M.S. degree at Department of Computer Science and Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2015 and 2018, respectively.
