Brief Introduction

Tianfan Fu is PhD candidate in Department of Computational Science and Engineering at Georgia Institute of Technology, supervised by Prof. Jimeng Sun. He received his B.S. and M.S. degree at Department of Computer Science and Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2015 and 2018, respectively.
